About KIRI HackJak

KIRI HackJak is an app to help people finding the nearest routes in Jakarta. This app participated in HackJak event in Jakarta, April 2014.

KIRI HackJak is made by:

  • Pascal Alfadian (KIRI)
  • Aristo Hadisoeganda (Starfe)
  • Ibnu Sina Wardy (GITS)

The data in this app is retrieved from public, available at these sites:

  • Open Data UKP4 (http://data.ukp.go.id/)
  • Peta Angkutan Umum (https://angkot.web.id)
